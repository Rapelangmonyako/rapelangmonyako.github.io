About Me

ABOUT ME

My name is Rapelang Monyako, a 27-year-old visual communications and advertising specialist. I studied advertising at Limkokwing University of Creative Technology. My design career began during my second year at university, when I landed my first job at Koko, the country's leading advertising agency at the time. Starting as a junior graphic designer during my internship, I quickly advanced to the role of Graphic Designer.

During my time at Koko, I had the privilege of designing and animating for some of the nation's most prominent brands, including Alliance Insurance Company, Econet Lesotho, Mpilo Boutique Hotel, and Standard Lesotho Bank. I became the designated designer for Standard Lesotho Bank and played a pivotal role in their renowned "Ha hona Stress" campaign.

After Koko, I moved to Revenue Services Lesotho, previously known as Lesotho Revenue Authority, where I worked as an Advertising Officer and later as an Advertising and Multimedia Design Officer. This role allowed me to be deeply involved in various campaigns, including the critical rebranding effort when the organization changed its name. My responsibilities included designing artwork and ensuring consistent application of the Brand Manual.

Currently, I am a Graphic Designer at Metropolitan Lesotho, where I am actively involved in executing the "Be Covered" campaign. My journey has been fueled by a passion for creativity and a commitment to delivering compelling visual narratives.
